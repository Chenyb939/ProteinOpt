    ProteinOpt: Protein Stability Optimization Tool

Manually Specified

ProteinOpt is a multi-objective optimization tool specifically designed for vaccine antigens and decoys, leveraging the Rosetta software suite and Snakemake. ProteinOpt is designed to assist researchers in conveniently and swiftly conducting protein stability optimization operations using local resources. ProteinOpt offers four distinct protein optimization methods, including:Point Mutation Scanning, RosettaVIP, Supercharge and Manually Specified Seed Residues.
